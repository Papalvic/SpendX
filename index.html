FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you download.

Is SpendX free to use? ▾
Yes, completely. SpendX is free to download and use with no subscription, no in-app purchases, and no hidden charges. All features are available from the moment you install it.
Does SpendX require an internet connection? ▾
No. SpendX works entirely offline. All analysis and predictions happen on your device. You only need internet to download the app itself — never to use it.
Is my bank statement uploaded anywhere? ▾
Absolutely not. Your statement is processed in-memory on your Android device and is never sent to any server, cloud service, or third party. SpendX has no backend that receives your data.
Which statement formats does SpendX support? ▾
SpendX currently supports OPay bank statement exports in PDF and CSV formats. Support for additional Nigerian banks is planned for future releases.
Is my financial data truly private? ▾
Yes. SpendX does not request any network permissions for data transmission. Your financial information stays on your device and is fully under your control. We believe your spending data is yours alone.
